<objective>
Add silent background prefetching on checkout step transitions to reduce perceived latency. While a customer types their address (30-120 seconds), prefetch the data their next step will need. Uses Phase 110's query key factory, the `useQueryClient()` hook, and SHARED queryFn exports (`menuQueryFn` from Plan 03, `addressesQueryFn` added here) — zero new visible UI, zero spinners, zero animations.

Free background work during the natural typing window. Critically, the prefetch and the consumer hook share the EXACT same queryFn reference so TanStack Query deduplicates perfectly and the cached data shape matches the consumer's expectations.

Add silent background prefetching on checkout step transitions to reduce perceived latency. While a customer types their address (30-120 seconds), prefetch the data their next step will need. - `CheckoutClient.tsx` with `useQueryClient()` + a `useEffect` watching `step` that prefetches the next step's data

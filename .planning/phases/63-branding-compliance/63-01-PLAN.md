   - Order data: delivery address, order history, dietary preferences
   - Payment info: processed by Stripe (we do not store card numbers)
   - Device/usage data: browser type, IP address, pages visited, referring URL

3. **How We Use Your Information** -- Order fulfillment, communication (order updates, transactional emails), service improvement, error diagnosis.

4. **Third-Party Service Providers** -- Name each explicitly:
   - **Google (OAuth):** We use Google Sign-In for account authentication. When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo. We request only standard scopes: openid, email, and profile.
   - **Stripe:** Payment processing. Your payment card details are sent directly to Stripe and never touch our servers. See Stripe's privacy policy.
   - **Resend:** We use Resend to deliver transactional emails (order confirmations, status updates). Resend processes your email address for delivery.
   - **Sentry:** Error monitoring and performance tracking. When errors occur, Sentry may capture anonymized session recordings to help us diagnose issues. All text, form inputs, and media are masked in these recordings (maskAllText, maskAllInputs, blockAllMedia). Session recordings are only captured when errors occur (replaysOnErrorSampleRate: 1.0, replaysSessionSampleRate: 0). Sentry also collects browser breadcrumbs (console, DOM interactions, network requests) for error context.
   - **Vercel:** Web analytics (Vercel Analytics) and performance monitoring (Vercel Speed Insights) to understand site usage and loading performance. Speed Insights operates at a 50% sample rate.

5. **Data Sharing** -- We share personal information ONLY with the service providers listed above. We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

6. **Data Retention** -- Account data retained while account is active. Order history retained for business records. You may request deletion at any time.

7. **Cookies and Similar Technologies** -- Session cookies for authentication, analytics cookies (Vercel), error monitoring cookies (Sentry). No cookie consent banner implemented yet (informational disclosure only -- do NOT claim consent mechanism exists).

8. **Your Rights** -- Right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Contact us at the email below.

9. **California Residents** -- "California residents may have additional rights under state law. Contact us at the email below for more information." (Brief acknowledgment, do NOT claim CCPA compliance.)

10. **Children's Privacy** -- Service not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

11. **Changes to This Policy** -- We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

12. **Contact Us** -- admin@mandalaymorningstar.com, physical address from KITCHEN_LOCATION constant (750 Terrado Plaza, Suite 33, Covina, CA 91723).
